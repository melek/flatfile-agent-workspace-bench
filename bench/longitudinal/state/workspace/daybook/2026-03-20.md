- **Commitment precision vs. timeline**
- **Blame locus** (we missed it vs. external changed)

Pivot-now assumes we can delta-change our approach mid-engagement without thrashing. Pause-rescope assumes the two weeks are worth the timeline cost and that we can successfully re-baseline and move faster after.
