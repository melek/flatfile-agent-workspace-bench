# Verdant Foods Retainer — Revised Scope Section

## 1. SCOPE OF WORK

### 1.1 In-Scope Deliverables

The following services are included within the fixed fee:

- **Data Extraction & Ingestion**
  - Ingestion of raw procurement data from Client's designated systems (format: CSV/Excel, schema provided by Client)
  - Single pass data validation against Client-provided specifications
  - Mapping of Client data to dashboard input schema
  - One (1) round of reconciliation for data quality issues identified during validation
  
- **Dashboard Build**
  - Development of procurement analytics dashboard per functional specification dated [INSERT DATE]
  - Deployment to three (3) production environments (Plant A, Plant B, Plant C)
  - Post-deployment validation: execution of Client-approved test cases
  - Configuration for up to 6 named power users (2 per plant, identified by Client)
  
- **Training**
  - Delivery of instructor-led training for identified power users (2 users per plant, 6 total)
  - Training duration: up to 4 hours per group
  - Training materials: slide deck, quick reference guide, recorded session
  - One (1) follow-up support session per group within 10 business days of go-live

### 1.2 Explicitly Out of Scope

The following are **not** included in this engagement and require a separate change order:

- Additional users beyond the 6 identified power users (e.g., ad-hoc training for non-power users)
- Modifications to the functional specification after [INSERT KICKOFF DATE] without documented change request
- Integration with systems other than those specified in the data extract specification
- Data cleaning, normalization, or transformation beyond the mapping described in Section 1.1
- Multiple rounds of data reconciliation (only one included; additional rounds billed at $X per hour)
- Custom reporting or dashboard modifications beyond the agreed-upon feature set
- Support or maintenance beyond the initial go-live support window (defined below)
- Travel, meals, or accommodation costs beyond those explicitly authorized in writing

### 1.3 Change Management

Any request for scope expansion, specification revision, or deliverable modification must be:
- Submitted in writing (email) to [Project Lead] with specific description
- Approved in writing by Client's authorized representative (Marcus or designee)
- Executed as a formal change order, including revised timeline and fee adjustment
- No work will proceed on out-of-scope requests until signed change order is in place

### 1.4 Timeline & Acceptance

- **Delivery Date:** May 17, 2026 (Week 6 from March 3 kickoff)
- **Client Acceptance:** Deliverables are deemed accepted 5 business days after delivery unless written objection is submitted
- **Acceptance Criteria:** 
  - Dashboard is accessible to all 6 power users
  - All core reports execute without error
  - Training completion confirmed by Client
