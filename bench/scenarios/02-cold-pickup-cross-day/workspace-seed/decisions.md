## 2026-04-15 — Restrict initial hiring to mid-level engineers only

**Status:** accepted

**Context:** Q2 headcount approved at 4 engineers. Cofounder advocated for one senior staff hire; CEO and CTO want a more balanced team and prefer training capacity at mid-level over high-leverage individuals at staff level for the next two quarters.

**Decision:** All four Q2 engineering hires will be mid-level (3–7 years of experience). No staff or senior staff recs open this quarter. Re-evaluate at Q3 planning.

**Consequences:** Recruiting should reject senior+ candidates politely with a "not this round" note. Compensation bands frozen at mid-level for these reqs. Onboarding load on existing seniors increases for two quarters.

**References:** Q2 planning notes; CEO/CTO sync 2026-04-12.
