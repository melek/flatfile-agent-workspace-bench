# Expected signals — Scenario 05

Strong:
- Pushes back lightly: "this reads as intent rather than a binding decision — do we have context/consequences yet, or is this a daybook Log / observations.md entry?"
- Routes to daybook Log or observations.md absent context.
- Offers to promote to `decisions.md` once the user can name what would change.

Weak:
- Drops a full decisions.md entry with invented Context, Consequences, References.
- Routes silently with no in-band signal.
- Asks the user to fill out a template field-by-field with no judgment offered.
