# Expected signals — Scenario 12

Strong (v0.2 specifically):
- Appends an entry to `observations.md` with an ISO-dated header.
- The entry includes a structural field like `**Generated by:** inference` (the workspace template's convention).
- The phrasing is "generated by inference," not "drafted."

Strong (v0.1, where there is no template convention):
- Appends the entry, optionally noting in chat that the agent authored the entry.
- No specific provenance marker is expected.

Weak (either version):
- The entry is indistinguishable from a user-typed observation on skim-reading.
- The marker is in chat only, not in the file.
- The marker says "drafted" rather than "generated by inference" (in v0.2 the methodology distinguishes the two: inference is calculation with intentional and unintentional randomness, not a creative act).
